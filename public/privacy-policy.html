Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Expense Track ("the Application", "we", "us", or "our") handles raw data, user information, and privacy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and security.

Core Privacy Principle: Offline-First
Expense Track is designed as a fully offline-first app. Your financial data, budgets, income logs, and category setups are stored locally on your physical device. We do not run automated background server syncing, nor do we host or manage your financial transaction data on any remote servers.

1. Information We Collect

Because the app runs locally on your device, we DO NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any of your financial data on remote servers. All entry profiles, transactions, and categories remain strictly inside your local device cache, IndexedDB, or standard state engines.

2. Third-Party Integrations & Scope

  • Google AdMob: The Application uses Google AdMob to deliver advertising. Google AdMob may collect and process device identifiers, advertising ID, location data, or standard telemetric diagnostics to personalize ads in accordance with Google Play developer policies and the Google privacy frameworks.
  • Google Drive Sync (Optional): If you explicitly choose to back up or restore your data via Google Drive, the App requests limited permission to store a secure backup file inside your private application-specific folder. This connection is direct between your device and Google Drive; no data is ever routed through our servers.

3. Local Storage & Data Deletion

Because your transaction records are saved entirely on your local machine or phone storage, deleting your web browser data, clearing app cache, or uninstalling Expense Track will permanently delete your stored data unless you have made a Google Drive backup. We have no access to your data, so you have absolute control over your deletion:

  • Local Data Deletion: You can purge all your local expense logs, budgets, and settings at any time by going to the App Settings and clicking "Reset All Data" or "Clear All Local Data", or by clearing your device's browser cache/app storage.
  • Google Drive Backup Deletion: If you created backups in Google Drive, you can delete them directly from your Google Drive account, or disconnect the app's authorization under your Google Account Security Settings.
  • Requesting Account Deletion: Since we do not create, collect, or store any user accounts or user data on private external database servers, there is no personal data held on our end to delete. However, if you have any questions or require assistance with manual data clearing, you may contact us at firstpayyourself@gmail.com.

4. Data Encryption in Transit

We care about securing any transit pathways. Any external requests performed by the Application—such as optional Google Drive syncing or Google AdMob services—are strictly established over secure HTTPS connections (SSL/TLS). This ensures that any data leaving your device to connect with external Google services is fully encrypted in transit.

5. Regulatory Compliance

  • GDPR & CCPA: Because no personal finance data is transmitted or stored by us, you retain complete sovereignty over your data. You can inspect, modify, or permanently delete your records directly from the App settings menu.
  • Children's Privacy (COPPA): The Application does not intentionally seek or collect information from children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy of Expense Track periodically. You are advised to review this page occasionally for any changes. Changes are effective immediately once updated on this URL.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy statement, you can reach out via email: firstpayyourself@gmail.com.
